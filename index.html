What people are looking at

This camera covers the coastal character of Vlore rather than one single static landmark. It works because the mood changes through the day: calmer mornings, brighter sea light, ferry and harbour movement, sunset colour and night reflections over the bay.

The stream is useful for two types of viewers: people checking Vlore as a travel destination, and people using the sea view as background ambience.

Main view Lungomare waterfront, Vlore Bay and the port atmosphere.
Best moments First light, evening glow, night reflections and weather changes over the sea.
