Ionian LiveCam - Vlore Port Webcam

Live webcam from Vlore, Albania, with views over Lungomare, Vlore Bay, the port area and Sazan Island.

Enjoy live views of the Ionian coast, sea light, harbour atmosphere and changing weather from one of the most scenic spots in Vlore.

At 05:40 the camera turns toward Sazan Island to catch the sunrise light.

About this live webcam

This live camera shows the Lungomare waterfront in Vlore, Albania, with a wide sea view across Vlore Bay toward Sazan Island and the surrounding Ionian coastline. The stream is designed for viewers who enjoy calm scenery, port activity, natural sea light, sunsets, night views and relaxing background ambience.

The camera regularly captures ferries, boats, changing weather, reflections on the water and the first light of the day. It is both a scenic travel webcam and a relaxing stream for people who like to watch the sea, study, unwind or fall asleep with a peaceful coastal view.
