What this camera shows

Ionian LiveCam focuses on the coastal rhythm of Vlore: the Lungomare waterfront, the broad line of Vlore Bay, harbour movement, changing weather and the distant shape of Sazan Island. The view works both as travel scenery and as a calm background stream for people who like sea ambience, night reflections and slow light changes.

Day and night contrast Sunrise, warm afternoon light, sunset colour and the soft glow of night reflections all give the stream different moods through the day.
Ambient viewing This format suits TV viewers, desk viewers and people using the stream for calm background atmosphere.
Travel value The page gives location context, map, weather and a direct route to the stream so it feels useful, not empty.
