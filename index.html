Ionian LiveCam – Vlore Port Webcam

Live webcam from Vlore, Albania showing Lungomare promenade, Vlore Bay and Sazan Island in the Ionian Sea.

At 05:40 the camera turns toward Sazan Island to capture the sunrise light over the sea.

This camera shows the coastal atmosphere of Vlore with sea views, harbour activity, changing weather, sunsets and night lights along the Lungomare waterfront.
